Background

Harry Potter was a young boy living with his aunt and uncle. Despite their best efforts, Harry's relatives cannot hide his magical abilities from him. Eventually, Harry meets Hargrid, a half-giant, who takes Harry to Hogwarts. Hogwarts is a school of witchcraft and wizardy. Harry slowly begins to learn more and more about his past. He learns that when he was just a baby, his parents were killed by an evil wizard named Voldemort. Harry is known as "the boy who lived" because Voldemort couldn't kill him as a baby.


Throughout the series, Harry becomes friends with many students at Hogwarts and begins to develop his magical powers. Harry learns more and more about his past, the secrets deep inside Hogwarts, and confronts his parent's murderer.
