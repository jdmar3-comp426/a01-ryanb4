Background

Accio

One of the basic spells that new wizards and witches learn. This spell summons objects to the caster.

Expecto Patronum

One of the most famous spells throughout the series. This spell produces a Patronous, which takes on the animal form of the caster. The patronous is used to scare away Dementors.

Expelliarmus

Spell that is aimed at an opponent. When an opponent is hit by the spell, they are forced to drop any object that they are holding.

Avada Kedavra

This is the most evil spell and it is forbidden to use. This curse kills the opponent it is cast upon. Harry is the only person to have survived this spell, when Voldemort used it on him as a baby.
