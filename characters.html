Background

Harry Potter

Main charcter throughout the series. You follow Harry from a child to a young man throughout the entire series.

Ron Weasley

Ron is one of Harry's best friends. Ron has a big family with five other siblings. Harry becomes another son to the Weasley's, since he doesn't have his parents anymore.

Hermione Granger

Hermione is another one of Harry's best friends. She first met Harry and Ron on the Hogwarts Express. She comes across as a "know it all" at first, but over time she learns how to better get along with others.
